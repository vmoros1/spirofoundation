About us

This illness, feared by everyone, comes from a genetic error in cells, which evolve in a sense contrary to the one they have been programmed for. The causes for such a dysfunction are numerous and not always known. Some are objective, others subjective, and some can be controlled.

Fear from the illness prompts people to make mistakes. The first and most frequent one is to enclose oneself in the illness. Before any treatment is undertaken, the absolute priority is to tame this fear, to calm it, we all have the solution within ourselves. In order to gain time, one must consider all possible options. One must keep an open mind, keep the body and spirit connected, and in this way our will can help the body to heal, and so remission can become recovery. Healthy eating habits, a well-regulated (settled) life and happiness can minimise cancer risks.
