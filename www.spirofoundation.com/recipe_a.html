The recipe contents:

*Half kilo of bees honey (Pure natural honey).
*40-50 ml (about 6 spoonfuls) of distillate (grappa, cognac or whisky).

*350 grams of ALOE ARBORESCENS leaves. The plant should be 3 years old; approximately one meter height.

The recipe preparation:
Remove the dust deposited on the leaves using a dry cloth or sponge; remove the thistles without removing the skin. Cut the leaves into small pieces and place them in the blender together with the honey and the chosen distillate. Blend well. Don’t filter. The blend obtained must be stored in the fridge in a dark jar with the lid tightly closed.

Recommended doses: 
Take one tablespoonful, 30 minutes before each of the three main meals, shake well before use.

After finishing the content of the jar (750 ml), wait 7 days then repeat the course of the treatment.
